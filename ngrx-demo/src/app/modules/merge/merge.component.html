Merge operator Example

When we have to collect data from diffrent stream and show it in single output field then we use Merge operator to handle such sitation. It generally used when we are not strict toward the sequence of data. it simply subscribe data from multiple obserable and parallely subcribe each and every obserable .

Ex -01

Animals ==> ['tiger','Bear','Lion','deer','zebra','fox']

Ex -02

Bird ==> ['pigeon','dove','cukoo','eagle','vulture','penguin']

Ex -03

Food ==> ['pizza','samosa','burger','idli','dosai','litti']

Here we can see the data coming from diffrent Stream are not in serial manner so that the data get mixed
